Plant It

A relaxing pixel art puzzle game where gardening meets thoughtful challenges. Each level is a small living canvas where you place plants wisely and shape a peaceful garden at your own pace.

Features

  • Relaxing puzzle gameplay inspired by gardening
  • Grow and arrange plants to complete handcrafted levels
  • Charming pixel art visuals and a calming atmosphere
  • No timers, no pressure, play offline anytime
  • Full creativity in Playground mode

Designed for calm, not stress, Plant It is a cozy alternative to fast-paced puzzle games. Plant, solve, and unwind.
