PlantIt Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 30, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to PlantIt ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our PlantIt mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

Important: PlantIt is designed with your privacy in mind. The app does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information to use its core features. The app does not require access to your location, camera, contacts, or any other sensitive device permissions. You can use PlantIt fully offline and anonymously. Any data you choose to provide remains on your device and under your control.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 No Required Personal Information

PlantIt does not require you to provide any personal information to use the app. You can use all core features of PlantIt without creating an account, logging in, or sharing any personal data. The app works completely offline and all your plant data is stored locally on your device.

2.2 Optional Account Information

If you choose to create an optional account for backup or sync features, we may collect:

  • Account Information: Email address (optional, only if you choose to create an account)
  • Profile Information: Username or display name (optional)

Note: Account creation is entirely optional and not required to use PlantIt.

2.3 Plant and Garden Data

Information you choose to store locally on your device:

  • Plant names, types, and species you track
  • Care schedules and reminders you set
  • Notes and observations about your plants
  • Photos of your plants and gardens (stored locally on your device)

Note: This data is stored locally on your device and is not automatically shared with us or any third parties.

2.4 No Location or Camera Requirements

PlantIt does not require access to your location, camera, or any other device sensors. If you choose to add photos to your plant records, you can select photos from your device's gallery without granting camera permissions. The app does not request or use location data.

2.5 Minimal Usage Information (Optional)

If you choose to enable anonymous usage statistics (opt-in), we may collect:

  • Anonymous app usage data (e.g., which features are used most often)
  • Crash reports and diagnostic information (anonymized, no personal data)

Note: Collection of usage statistics is optional and opt-in. You can disable this at any time in the app settings. This information is collected anonymously and cannot be linked to you personally.

3. How We Use Your Information

Since PlantIt does not require personal information and stores data locally on your device, we use minimal information only for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To provide the core plant tracking features that run entirely on your device
  • Optional Sync: If you create an optional account, to sync your data across devices
  • Improvement: To analyze anonymous usage patterns and improve app features and performance
  • Technical Support: To diagnose and fix technical issues when you report problems
  • Security: To detect and prevent technical issues and security threats

Note: We do not use your information for advertising, marketing, or selling to third parties.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. Since PlantIt stores your data locally on your device and does not require personal information, there is minimal information to share. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: Only when you explicitly choose to share information (e.g., exporting your plant data)
  • Anonymous Analytics: If you opt in to anonymous usage statistics, this data may be processed by analytics services to help us improve the app. This data is fully anonymized and cannot be linked to you or your device.
  • Optional Cloud Sync: If you create an optional account and enable sync, your encrypted plant data is stored with our cloud hosting provider
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety

Note: We do not share your plant data, photos, or any personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Your data remains private and under your control.

5. Data Security

Your privacy and security are our top priorities. Since PlantIt stores your data locally on your device and does not require you to send personal information to our servers, your data remains under your control.

  • Local Storage: Your plant data, photos, and notes are stored securely on your device
  • No Account Required: You can use the app without creating an account or sharing personal information
  • Optional Encryption: If you use the optional sync feature, data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Minimal Data Collection: We only collect anonymous usage statistics that cannot identify you

However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to keep your device secure and use device-level security features like passwords or biometric authentication.

6. Data Retention

Since PlantIt stores your data locally on your device, you have complete control over your data retention. Your plant records, photos, and notes remain on your device until you choose to delete them. We do not store your personal plant data on our servers unless you opt to use the optional sync feature.

If you use the optional sync feature and create an account, your synced data will be retained until you delete your account or remove specific data. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the app settings.

Anonymous usage statistics are retained for analytical purposes but cannot be linked to you personally.

7. Your Rights and Choices

With PlantIt, you have complete control over your data:

  • Full Control: Your data is stored on your device and you can access, modify, or delete it at any time through the app
  • No Account Required: You can use PlantIt without creating an account, meaning you don't need to provide any personal information
  • Export Data: You can export your plant data at any time in a portable format
  • Delete Data: You can delete individual plant records or all your data directly from your device
  • Opt-Out of Analytics: You can disable anonymous usage statistics in the app settings
  • Account Deletion: If you created an optional account, you can delete it and all synced data at any time

Since your data is stored locally on your device, you have immediate control without needing to contact us. For questions about privacy, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact section below.

8. Children's Privacy

PlantIt is safe for users of all ages. Since the app does not require account creation, does not collect personal information, and stores all data locally on the device, it can be safely used by children under the supervision of parents or guardians. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and have concerns about your child's use of the app, please contact us.

9. Third-Party Links and Services

PlantIt is designed to work offline and does not require internet connectivity or access to third-party services for its core functionality. However, if you choose to use optional features such as exporting data or accessing help documentation, you may encounter links to external resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of any external websites or services you choose to visit.

10. International Data Transfers

Since PlantIt stores your data locally on your device by default, there are no international data transfers for the core app functionality. However, if you choose to use the optional sync feature and create an account, your encrypted data may be stored on servers that could be located outside of your country. Data stored on sync servers is encrypted and you can delete it at any time. By opting to use the sync feature, you consent to this optional transfer of your encrypted data.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
