Plant It Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 30, 2025

Simple Privacy Statement:

Plant It is a simple puzzle game that does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The game does not require any permissions to access your camera, location, contacts, or any other personal data. You can enjoy the game completely offline with full privacy.

Information Collection

Plant It does not collect any personal information from users. The game operates entirely on your device and does not require an internet connection. We do not collect, transmit, or store any data that could identify you personally.

Permissions

Plant It does not request or require any device permissions. The game does not access your:

• Camera
• Location
• Contacts
• Photos or media files
• Microphone
• Any other personal information or device features

Data Storage

Any game progress or settings are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers. We have no access to this information.

Third-Party Services

Plant It does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The game is completely self-contained and does not connect to external services.

Children's Privacy

Plant It is safe for users of all ages, including children. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no privacy concerns for children using the app.

Changes to Privacy Policy

If we make any changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update this page with the new policy and update the "Last Updated" date. We recommend checking this page periodically for any updates.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@plantit.app
